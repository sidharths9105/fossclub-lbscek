About Us

The FOSS Club at LBS College of Engineering Kasaragod is dedicated to promoting and advancing the use of Free and Open Source Software. We believe in the power of collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing.

Our mission is to:

  • Promote awareness about FOSS
  • Organize workshops and training sessions
  • Contribute to open source projects
  • Build a community of FOSS enthusiasts
